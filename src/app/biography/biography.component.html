Sam Fly is an LA/NYC based vocalist currently singing duets on tour with Grammy award-winning artist Michael Bolton.

Sam began her career almost 15 years ago in New York City. At the start, Sam was heavily interested in stage acting, with becoming a Broadway star as her main goal. She starred in many musicals including Beauty and the Beast, The Wizard of Oz, Bye Bye Birdie, and A Chorus Line. As she got older and her career progressed, she realized that her variety of talents could take her down other roads in the performing arts. Her off-broadway career eventually led her into the music industry. Sam utilized her training in classical opera to assist her in pivoting between multiple genres of music.

In 2012, Sam co-starred in a television series, The Next Big Thing: NY, that was picked up by the Oxygen Network. The show aired that summer in the U.S. and starting in early 2013, on many of the international markets of E! Entertainment.
